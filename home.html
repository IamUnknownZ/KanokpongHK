BOY'S STORY

Season 1: Origins

Chapter 1: See the World

10th October 2005 — a boy is born in Thailand. A quiet beginning, before the world starts to feel bigger every year.

Chapter 2: The Learning Years
  • Started kindergarten at Christian Borikan Thonburi School.
  • Then studied primary school at Anurajaprasit School.
  • Later continued to secondary school at Triam Udom Suksa Pattanakarn Nonthaburi School.
Chapter 3: The World Went Quiet
Around 2019, the world changed. Studying from home made life smaller — but curiosity bigger. With more time online, he explored videos, forums, tutorials, and anything that sparked interest in tech and games.
Chapter 4: Goodbye, Old Days
He graduated from high school in the Science–Computer program. That path made computer subjects feel less like “schoolwork” and more like a real direction.
Chapter 5: New Campus, New Crew
The boy isn’t a boy anymore — he became a full-time university student. He enrolled in Computer Education, Faculty of Technical Education, King Mongkut’s University of Technology North Bangkok. New friends, new routines, and new skills — every week feels like leveling up.
Chapter 6: The Next Episode
The story is still in progress — learning, building, improving, and failing on purpose. The next episode will be bigger, smarter, and more “him”.
