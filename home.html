Season 1: Origins

2005 — I was born
Chapter 1: See the world

"10th October 2005, a boy is born in a big city in Thailand. Little did he know, his journey into the world was about to begin."

2018 — The Spark
Chapter 2: Hello World

The first time I typed “Hello, World” felt like unlocking a secret door. It wasn’t about the code being perfect — it was the feeling that I could create something from nothing. From that day on, I kept learning, experimenting, breaking things, and trying again… because I knew this was the path I wanted.

2023 — Graduation
Chapter 3: Graduation 2023

In 2023, I completed my compulsory education and graduated from Triam Udom Suksa Pattanakarn Nonthaburi School, Science–Computer program. It was a turning point — proof that I could stay consistent, learn under pressure, and keep moving toward the future I wanted in tech.

2024 — First Real Project
Chapter 4: First Real Project

This was the first time I built something that felt real — not just a practice file. I learned how to plan, design, and finish what I started, even when it got messy.

2025 — Leveling Up
Chapter 5: Leveling Up

I began taking my skills seriously: cleaner code, better structure, and more confidence. Every mistake became a lesson, and every lesson became momentum.

Now — The Next Episode
Chapter 6: The Next Episode

The story is still going. New goals, new challenges, and bigger dreams — I’m ready for whatever comes next.
